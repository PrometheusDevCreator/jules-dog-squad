Who We Are

Meet Jules & Riley

The human-canine dream team behind every muddy, joyful, tail-wagging adventure across South Manchester.

Every dog deserves to feel like part of the pack.

Jules has been walking dogs professionally across the Chorlton area for years, building a loyal squad of four-legged regulars who know the fields, paths and puddles of South Manchester better than most humans.

With Riley leading the charge (usually towards the nearest mud), every walk is a proper adventure. Whether it's a frosty morning at Turn Moss, a sunny romp through Chorlton Ees, or a rainy plod through Longford Park, the squad gets out in all conditions — because happy dogs don't check the weather forecast.

Small group sizes mean every dog gets proper attention, socialisation with their mates, and the kind of exercise that leaves them snoozing contentedly until teatime.

What We Offer

Services

From group adventures to solo strolls, we've got your dog covered — come rain, shine, or sideways Manchester drizzle.

Group Walk

The full squad experience. Your dog joins a small group of compatible pals for a proper 60-minute adventure across our favourite green spaces. Maximum 5 dogs ensures everyone gets attention and space to explore.

£15 / walk

Solo Walk

One-on-one time for dogs who prefer their own company, need extra attention, or are still building confidence. A dedicated 45-minute walk at your dog's own pace and comfort level.

£20 / walk

Pop-In Visit

A 30-minute home visit for toilet breaks, feeding, a bit of company and a garden potter. Ideal for puppies, older dogs, or if you're stuck at work a bit longer than planned.

£12 / visit
Where We Walk

Our Stomping Grounds

Three brilliant green spaces that the squad knows inside out — every shortcut, every good sniffing spot, every puddle worth jumping in.

Turn Moss

Wide open playing fields perfect for a proper run-about. Frosty mornings here are magical — the squad loves it when the grass crunches underfoot.

Chorlton Ees

Meadows, woodland and the River Mersey. Proper countryside feel right in the city. Muddy paths guaranteed — bring your sense of adventure (and a towel).

Longford Park

A beautiful Victorian park with mature trees, gentle paths and plenty of friendly dogs to say hello to. Great for calmer walks and senior pups.
